Biography

Jonas Schledorn was born and raised around the Ruhr Area in Western Germany. After graduating from media school in 2021, he moved to cologne to study photography at the Fotoakademie-Koeln. He works within the social documentary practice, covering the struggles of everyday life. The goal is, to create a connection to the subject which is based on eye level.
